about me

Tireless and Flexible

As a student in Skillcrush, I've completed courses in HTML, CSS Flexbox, PHP, Wordpress and web design, and am well equipped to produce custom, modern websites. I'm a great match for small business owners looking to update their site or create one from scratch.

I have some unique past experience in marketing and the arts. With a local solar company, I rose from door to door marketer to Director of Outside Marketing, in charge of 80 representatives. My teams accounted for 95% of the leads generated in the first five years, those leads accounted for 750k in gross sales year 1 and grew to 35 million in year 5. I attribute my performing arts experience to this success. The ability to take direction, connect with humans and perform under pressure in a creative environment on strict deadlines are translatable skills to almost any profession.

Why hire a web developer in training? Value. I'm currently able to offer high quality work at extremely competitive rates in order to supply my portfolio with shining examples and references.

When the pandemic hit I was pursuing my passion as a union stage actor and performer. With no end in site I decided a career in tech would be a valuable and safe alternative career path. Luckily, after diving in it has turned into a new passion. I'm motivated in helping others adapt to our new reality as our priorities change and we put more focus online. I'm also motivated by our growing interest in social change, and will be prioritizing clients who share ethical humanist and progressive values. When we're not in the middle of a global pandemic, you can typically find me groovin on the dance floor, singing karaoke or adventuring the great outdoors.
